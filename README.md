# customer-segmentation-
This repository contains an end-to-end workflow for customer segmentation leveraging unsupervised machine learning models. The goal is to identify distinct customer groups based on behavioral and demographic data, enabling businesses to tailor marketing strategies, improve customer experience, and drive growth 
The data set i use here is from kaggle-https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vishakhdapat/customer-segmentation-clustering
- Data Preprocessing
Comprehensive preparation of raw customer datasets, including handling missing values, outlier detection, normalization, and feature engineering. This ensures the data is clean, consistent, and suitable for clustering, while also highlighting the most relevant attributes (e.g., spending score, frequency of visits, demographics).
- Clustering Models
Implementation of multiple clustering algorithms such as K-Means, Hierarchical Clustering, and DBSCAN. Each model is applied to discover natural groupings within the customer base, with comparisons drawn to understand which algorithm best captures the underlying structure of the data.
- Evaluation Metrics
Rigorous evaluation using Silhouette Score, Davies–Bouldin Index, and  to determine the optimal number of clusters and assess the quality of segmentation. These metrics provide quantitative evidence that the clusters are meaningful and well-separated.
- Business Insights
Beyond technical clustering, the repository emphasizes translating results into actionable business strategies. For example, identifying high-value customers for loyalty programs, recognizing budget-conscious segments for discount campaigns, or tailoring product recommendations to specific lifestyle clusters.
- Visualization
Clear and intuitive visualizations using Matplotlib, Seaborn, and interactive dashboards. Cluster distributions, customer profiles, and comparative plots make it easy for stakeholders to interpret results and connect data-driven insights to real-world business contexts.
